ML Mux LED driver

The led_mlmux is a LED driver that sends LED settings
to LED component on EC over mlmux channel. The settings
are programmed into the hardware by the LED driver on
the EC.  When the settings are read from sysfs, the
values are retrieved from LED component on EC.

Required properties:
- compatible: "ml,leds_mlmux"

Example:
mlmux-leds {
	compatible = "ml,leds_mlmux";
	mlmux = <&mux>;
};
